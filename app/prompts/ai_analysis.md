# AI Analysis Prompt

## Task
Perform comprehensive analysis of candidates against job requirements, providing detailed insights and recommendations for hiring decisions.

## Analysis Components

### 1. Candidate-Job Fit Assessment
- Evaluate overall match between candidate profile and job requirements
- Assess technical skills alignment
- Analyze experience level appropriateness
- Review career progression compatibility

### 2. Strengths Identification
- Highlight candidate's strongest qualifications
- Identify unique value propositions
- Note exceptional achievements or experiences
- Recognize transferable skills and potential

### 3. Concerns and Gaps Analysis
- Identify potential concerns or red flags
- Assess skill or experience gaps
- Evaluate cultural fit indicators
- Consider development needs and timeline

### 4. Recommendation Framework
- Provide clear hire/no-hire recommendation with confidence level
- Suggest specific next steps (interview, additional screening, rejection)
- Recommend interview focus areas
- Propose compensation range considerations

## Output Format
Provide analysis in structured markdown format with:
- **Overall Assessment**: High-level fit summary
- **Key Strengths**: Bullet points of strongest qualifications
- **Potential Concerns**: Areas requiring further investigation
- **Recommendation**: Clear hiring decision guidance
- **Interview Focus**: Suggested discussion topics

## Evaluation Criteria
- Use evidence-based assessments from resume data
- Balance objective qualifications with subjective fit factors
- Consider both current capabilities and growth potential
- Maintain fairness and avoid biased assumptions
- Provide actionable insights for decision making
